About Me

I am a physicist and engineer, currently based in London, UK. My background is in quantum physics and quantum photonics; the study of the quantum mechanical nature of light. I work on technologies using light. In the past I've worked on photonic quantum computing, at the intersection between physics, engineering and computer science.
