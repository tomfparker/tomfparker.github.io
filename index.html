About Me

I am researcher and physicist (and kind of an engineer, but not quite). My background is in quantum optics and photonics; the study of the quantum mechanical nature of light.

Now I'm working in industry to try to build a photonic quantum computer; a machine that uses single particles of light to do computations.

More generally, I'm interested in the intersection between computing and photonics, and whether specialised photonic hardware can be used for bespoke computing applications.
